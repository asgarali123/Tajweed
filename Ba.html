Letter Baa (ب) - Tajweed Rules

This video explains the proper pronunciation of the letter Baa (ب).

Makhraj of Baa (ب)

The letter (ب) uses the lips to articulate its sound.

  • (ب) is pronounced with the wet portions of the lips.
  • This falls under Huroofush-Shafawiyyah The letter (حروف الشفويه)

Mouth Formation for the Letter Baa (ب)

  • The lip joins together, sealing the air then releasing it.
  • It is also a light letter so your mouth is flat when pronouncing it.

Sifaat (Characteristics)

  • Qalqalah: Echoing sound
  • Jahr: Clear sound.
  • Shiddah: Complete stoppage of sound.
  • Istifal: Tongue remains flat.
  • Infitaah: Sound is between the tongue and the palate.
  • Idhlaq: Pronounced with ease and quickly

Tajweed Rules

  • Mustafilah: If before the ب there is an ا, then it would be read with muraqqaq (empty mouth)
  • Ikhfaa As-Shafawi (with ghunnah): When applying Ikhfaa Shafawi, you should not fully pronounce the "Meem" sound, but rather let it slightly fade into the "Baa" sound while maintaining a slight lip closure./li>

NB: There is disagreement among scholars about how to perform this Ikhfaa, so you have to, follow your own Teacher.
