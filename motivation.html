Why Learn to Read the Qur'an?

A journey to connect with the words of Allah and strengthen your faith.

Direct Access to the Qur'an

Learning to read the Qur'an in its original Arabic brings you closer to Allah's message. It allows you to recite His words accurately, understand the blessings of reading, and fulfill your religious duty with confidence.

Improved Worship

Accurate recitation enhances your Salah (prayers) and overall worship. The Qur'an’s proper pronunciation (Tajweed) ensures that your recitation is correct, beautiful, and spiritually uplifting.

Spiritual Connection

Reading the Qur'an builds a deeper spiritual bond with Allah. It strengthens your faith, increases your love for the Qur'an, and provides peace and guidance in your daily life.

Rewards and Blessings

The act of reading the Qur'an, even with effort, carries immense rewards. Each letter you recite earns you blessings, and your dedication is beloved to Allah, no matter your skill level.

7 Hadith on the Qur'an

1. The Best of You

خيرُكم من تعلم القرآن وعلمهُ

"The best of you are those who learn the Qur'an and teach it."

[Bukhari]

2. Intercessor on the Day of Judgment

اقروءوا القرآن فإنه يأتي يوم القيامة شفيعا لأصحابه

"Read the Qur'an, for it will come as an intercessor for its reciters on the Day of Judgment."

[Muslim]

3. The Reward for Reciting

من قرأ حرفًا من كتاب الله فله به حسنة والحسنة بعشرة أمثالها

"Whoever recites a letter from the Book of Allah will receive a reward, and the reward will be multiplied tenfold."

[Tirmidhi]

4. Light for the Believer

القرآن نورٌ للمسلم في الدنيا والآخرة

"The Qur'an is light for the believer in this world and the Hereafter."

5. Elevation through the Qur'an

يرفعُ اللهُ بهذا الكتاب أقوامًا ويضعُ بهِ آخرين

"Indeed, Allah raises through this Book some people, and lowers others."

[Muslim]

6. Protection from Hellfire

لو أنزلنا هذا القرآن على جبلٍ

"If this Qur'an were to be sent down upon a mountain, you would have seen it humbled and splitting apart from the fear of Allah."

[Qur'an 59:21]

7. Qur'an in the Heart

مثلُ القرآن في صدر المسلم كمثلـــه

"The example of a believer who recites the Qur'an is like a citrus fruit, which tastes good and smells good."

[Bukhari]
