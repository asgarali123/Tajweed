Rules

TAJWEED

TAJWEED

Tajweed means to recite every letter correctly, from its Makhraj with all its qualities. Its purpose is to recite the Quran in the manner it was revealed to and recited by our beloved Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. It is compulsory to learn.

Major Errors

Major Errors

  1. Reciting a letter in place of another
  2. Adding a letter to a word
  3. Deleting a letter from a word
  4. Reciting a vowel in place of another vowel
  5. Reciting a vowel in place of a sukoon

Minor Errors

Minor Errors

Leaving out any rule (by mistake) which beautifies the sound of the Quran.

Istiaadha

The rules of Istiaadha

Before reciting the Quran it is necessary to recite istiaadha.

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّـهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ

Basmalah

The rules of Basmalah

When starting a surah it will be necessary to recite basmalah as well.

بِسْمِ اللَّـهِ الرَّحْمَـٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Istiaadha, Basmalah & Surah

Istiaadha, Basmalah & Surah

When reciting istiaadha basmallah and the beginning of a surah could be done in four ways.

  • Wasl Kul
  • Wasl Awwal Fasl Thani
  • Fasl Awwal Wasl Thani
  • Fasl Kul

Jowf

Jowf

It is from the emptiness of the mouth and throat.

There are 3 letters that jowf occurs in: Alif, Waaw, and Yaa. These are part of Huruful Madd.
